1. Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba

As one of the hottest anime, Demon Slayer: Kimetsu no Yaiba should be picked up, which is what you should watch on Crunchyroll, of course. The dark-fantasy anime, originally published as a manga between 2016 and 2020, centers on demon slayers fighting against evil demons. The demon slayer characters including Tanjiro, Zenitsu and Inosuke look Samurai. There are powerful battles scenes by them. The first season of the anime, released in 2019, consists of 26 episodes.

2. My Hero Academia

If you like superhero anime, My Hero Academia is one of the most recommended anime to watch on Crunchyroll. The modern action anime series consists of the current four seasons with 83 episodes and the fifth season will be released in 2021. My Hero Academia focuses on a young boy named Izuku Miroriya, and the growth story of him and his friends are depicted as heroes in the anime.

3.Jujutsu Kaisen

Jujutsu Kaisen (or “Sorcery Fight") is a Japanese manga series written and illustrated by Gege Akutami, serialized in Shueisha's Weekly Shōnen Jump since March 2018. The individual chapters are collected and published by Shueisha, with fifteen tankōbon volumes released as of March 2021. It follows high school student Yuji Itadori as he joins a secret organization of sorcerers in order to kill a powerful Curse named Ryomen Sukuna, of whom Yuji becomes the host. The manga is licensed for English release in North America by Viz Media and publishes it in print from December 2019. Shueisha publishes the series in English on the Manga Plus app and website. Two light novels written by Ballad Kitaguni have been released under the Jump J-Books imprint on May 2019 and January 2020, respectively. A 24-episode anime television series adaptation produced by MAPPA premiered on MBS in October 2020. The anime is licensed by Crunchyroll for streaming outside of Asia, who premiered an English dub in November 2020. As of March 2021, the Jujutsu Kaisen manga had over 36 million copies in circulation, including digital versions, making it one of the best-selling manga series of all time.

Japanese name: 呪術廻戦

4. Dr. Stone

Dr. Stone is a popular ongoing Shonen Jump manga and anime series. There are 24 episodes in the first season of the anime you, followed by the second season in 2021. The setting is 3700 years later than the modern time. The fantasy and adventure anime centers on a high school boy Senku Ishigami, who survives the primitive Stone World with the knowledge of science.

Japanese name: ドクターストーン

5. One-Punch Man

For anime fans who want to enjoy intense battles with humorous stories, One-Punch Man is highly recommended to watch on Crunchyroll. The action comedy anime has been one of the most famous modern manga and anime series outside Japan since the anime adaption was released in 2015. Now, there are 24 episodes in the two seasons of the anime adaption. One-Punch Man centers on the main character superhero named Saitama who never looks cool but can beat any enemy with a single punch.
